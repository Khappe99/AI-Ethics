Speaker Details

Caitlin Begg

Caitlin Begg is the founder of Authentic Social and a sociological researcher. She founded Authentic Social in 2016 after writing her undergraduate Harvard sociology honors thesis on virtual impressions and digital communication's effect on social interaction.

Authentic Social brings an education-forward approach to sociotechnical systems strategy, focused on social selling, communication, go-to-market and people operations. Caitlin presents sociological research on technology's effect on conversation around the U.S. and Europe, most recently at the Responsible Artificial Intelligence Forum in Munich.

Her professional and philanthropic efforts have been featured in Forbes, the Wall Street Journal, and Entrepreneur.

Join us for a transformative exploration of AI ethics, featuring practical sessions with Dr. Todd A. Jacobs and industry leaders.
